# 🍽️ Eating Out vs Health Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between **eating-out habits** and **health indicators** using Power BI.  
The dashboard provides insights into how lifestyle choices like food preferences, calorie intake, and exercise impact health outcomes such as BMI, cholesterol, and disease risk.


## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze eating-out frequency and its impact on health
- Identify high-risk groups based on BMI and cholesterol
- Compare health scores across demographics
- Understand lifestyle patterns affecting health


## 📁 Dataset Features
The dataset includes the following attributes:

- Age  
- Gender  
- Occupation  
- Eat_Out_Frequency  
- Preferred_EatOut_Type  
- Daily_Calories  
- Exercise_Hours_Per_Week  
- Sleep_Hours  
- BMI  
- Health_Score  
- Cholesterol_Level  
- BloodPressure  
- Diabetes  


## 📌 Key Metrics (DAX Measures)
- **Avg Eat-Out Frequency**
- **Average Health Score**
- **Diabetes %**
- **Hypertension %**
- **Average BMI**
- **High Risk Count**
- **Eat-Out Impact Score**


## 📊 Dashboard

## Slicers

## 📈 Key Insights
- Eating out **5+ times/week** is linked to **higher BMI and cholesterol**
- **Fast food consumers** tend to have **lower health scores**
- Individuals exercising **>4 hours/week** show better health outcomes
- **Students and young professionals** eat out more frequently and have poorer sleep patterns

This dashboard helps identify how eating habits influence health and highlights key risk factors, enabling better lifestyle decisions.
